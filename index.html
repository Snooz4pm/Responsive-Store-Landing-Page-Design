Our Story

Since 1995, Gusto Italiano has been serving authentic Italian cuisine made from family recipes passed down through generations.

Our chefs use only the finest imported ingredients from Italy, combined with local fresh produce to create dishes that honor traditional Italian cooking while adding a contemporary touch.

We believe in the Italian philosophy of "la dolce vita" - the sweet life - and strive to create an atmosphere where our guests can relax, enjoy good food, and make lasting memories.
